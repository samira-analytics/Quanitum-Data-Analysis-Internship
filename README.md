QVI Chip Purchasing Analysis - Retail Strategy & Analytics

This project involves a comprehensive analysis of customer chip purchasing behavior for a retail client. The objective is to identify key purchasing trends and customer segments to provide data-driven strategic recommendations for an upcoming category review.

Project Overview

The analysis is focused on understanding the "who" and "why" behind chip purchases. By examining transaction data and customer demographics, we define metrics that describe purchasing behavior and identify the most profitable customer segments.

Key Objectives:

•
Data Cleaning & Exploration: Process raw transaction and customer data, handle outliers, and engineer features like pack size and brand names.

•
Segment Analysis: Analyze total sales, customer counts, and average spend across different lifestages and premium customer tiers.

•
Deep Dive Insights: Investigate brand and pack size preferences for specific high-value segments.

•
Strategic Recommendations: Formulate actionable commercial strategies for the Category Manager.

Data Description

The analysis utilizes two primary datasets:

1.
Transaction Data: Contains transaction-level details including date, store number, loyalty card number, product name, quantity, and total sales.

2.
Purchase Behaviour: Contains customer-level information including lifestage (e.g., Young Families, Retirees) and premium status (Budget, Mainstream, Premium).

Key Findings

Metric
Insight
Top Sales Segment
Older Families - Budget drives the highest total volume and sales.
High-Value Growth
Young Singles/Couples - Mainstream show a high affinity for premium brands and larger pack sizes.
Brand Preference
Mainstream young customers prefer brands like Tyrrells, Twisties, and Doritos.
Pack Size
There is a significant preference for 270g and 380g packs among the target mainstream segments.




Strategic Recommendations

1.
Volume Retention: Implement value-based promotions for "Older Families - Budget" to maintain the high volume of sales.

2.
Premium Targeting: Focus marketing efforts for premium brands (Tyrrells, Kettle) on "Young Singles/Couples - Mainstream" via digital channels.

3.
Assortment Optimization: Ensure larger pack sizes (270g+) are prominently displayed in stores with high younger-demographic traffic.

4.
New Family Investigation: Conduct further research into the "New Families" segment to understand their lower-than-expected chip consumption.
